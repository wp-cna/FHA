From around Fisher Hill.

Post to the board

Share something with the neighborhood.

A place for neighbors to find each other and find what's nearby — local businesses and services, lost-and-found pets, tag sales and giveaways, and neighbor-to-neighbor needs and offers. Every submission is reviewed before it appears.

Welcome on the board

  • Local businesses, shops, and services
  • Lost & found — pets or items
  • Tag sales, yard sales, and giveaways
  • Neighbor-to-neighbor needs, offers, and giveaways
  • Civil and truthful, with your own contact info

Not allowed

  • Spam — bulk, repeated, automated, or bot submissions
  • Scams or fraudulent offers
  • Naming, targeting, or disparaging a specific individual
  • Harassing, threatening, or defamatory language
  • Reviews, ratings, or rankings of businesses — good or bad (this isn't Yelp)
  • Airing a personal dispute between neighbors
  • Sharing someone else's personal information without their consent
  • Anything unrelated to Fisher Hill or White Plains

Submissions are reviewed before posting. Submitting does not guarantee publication.
